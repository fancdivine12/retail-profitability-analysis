# 📊 Retail Profitability Analysis

## 🧰 Tools  
Excel

---

## 🧠 Business Problem  
Identify which regions, product categories, and customer segments drive sales and profitability, and determine why certain areas underperform.

---

## 📦 Data Preparation  
Prepared and structured retail sales and profit data for analysis, ensuring consistency and accuracy.

---

## 🧹 Data Processing  
Cleaned the dataset in Excel by handling missing values, removing inconsistencies, and standardizing formats.

---

## 🔍 Analysis  
Used pivot tables to evaluate performance across regions, categories, and sub-categories to identify profit drivers.

---

## 📊 Key Insights  

- Certain product categories generated high sales but low profitability  
- Tables and bookcases were identified as key drivers of profit loss  
- Profitability varied across regions  
- High sales volume did not always result in strong profit margins  

---

## 🚀 Business Impact  

These insights can guide pricing optimization, improve product focus, and support targeted regional investment to increase profitability.
